About Me

My name is Md. Anik Hasan and I have completed my Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET). Currently, I am serving as a Lecturer at Northern University Bangladesh, where I am actively involved in teaching and research.

My primary research interests include Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Biomedical Signal Processing (EEG, ECG, PPG, MRI), Large Language Models (LLM), Natural Language Processing (NLP), Computer Vision, and Reinforcement Learning.

During my academic career, I significantly contributed to research projects such as the "Driver Drowsiness Detection System using EEG signals," where I specialized in designing lightweight artificial neural networks and analyzing EEG data for biomarker extraction.
